Gili Petelu and Pink Beach Snorkeling Tour in Lombok



One Day Trip Pink Beach Lombok


One day trip Pink Beach Lombok has been put together for those of you who only have a limited amount of time and are visiting the island of Lombok.
With a trip length of up to 12 hours, you may spend 1 day exploring one of Lombok’s most popular tourist spots.
What are you waiting for? Read the entire description below and then contact us to place an order.


One Day Trip Pink Beach Lombok Itinerary



08.00 We will pick you up at your accommodation in Senggigi or Kuta Lombok. For those of you who wish to go for a stroll as soon as you arrive in Lombok, you can pick up in the morning at Lombok International Airport.

08.30 The journey continues on land to Tanjung Luar Harbor in East Lombok, where the Pink Beach Lombok crossing pier is located.

10.30 When you arrive at Tanjung Luar Harbor, our guide will instruct you on how to prepare your equipment, particularly snorkeling gear, before boarding the boat together.

11.00 Arrive at Segui Beach, also known as Pink Beach 2, and take photographs of its natural beauty.

12.00 Visit Pink Beach Lombok, also known as Tangsi Beach. At beautiful Pink Beach, lunch is offered. After that, you can take in the natural beauty of the area by climbing to the top of the tiny hill on the beach.

14.00 Go to Gili Petelu and snorkel to see the island’s underwater splendor.

15.30 Visit Pasir Island, a tidal island that lowers at high tide and floats at low tide.

16.30 Return to Tanjung Luar Harbor.

17.00 Arrive in Tanjung Luar Harbor, rinse, and continue your journey back to your Kuta Lombok or Senggigi Area accommodation.

19.00 Arrival time in Kuta or Senggigi is estimated. If you want to eat dinner, our guide can pick a restaurant based on your preferences and transport you there before returning to the hotel. Dinner is on your own dime.

20.00 The program is now complete.

*The above itinerary is flexible; our guide will assist you in planning a more optimal and pleasurable vacation.




Service Included :



  • AC transportation from your hotel to Tanjung Luar Port
  • Private boat during the Pink Lombok Beach Tour Packages
  • Snorkeling equipment
  • Entrance ticket to tourist area
  • Mineral water during the program
