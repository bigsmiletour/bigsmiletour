Snorkling Gili Nanggu




Full-day tour to Gili Nanggu Southwest of Lombok island



Snorkeling Gili Nanggu Lombok is the best daily tour activity to explore the southwest side of Lombok Island. Most of the island offer beautiful, quiet and calm crystal clear waters.

When most of the people hear take have a plant to visit about the Gili islands in Indonesia, they probably think of the 3 famous Gili islands Gili Trawangan, Gili Meno, and Gili Air in the northwest of Lombok. But there are many more Gili islands that are even more pretty and beautiful!

We create full-day trip to Gili Nanggu on the Southwest of Lombok island with No traffic, no crowds, and no vendors, but just unexploited nature and plenty of tropical fish.

This tour is offered as a full day tour that starts after your breakfast at your hotel. Hotel pick up is at 8:00 AM from the hotel lobby.

From the hotel we go on a 1:30 hour drive to Lembar Harbor or Tawun Harbor depend your place . From Lembar harbor we sail for about 20 minutes to reach Gili Nanggu.




Places to visit at Snorkeling Gili Nanggu Lombok :



  • Gili Nanggu
  • Gili Sudak
  • Gili Kedis


    • The tour of the three locations includes Equipment snorkeling to see the underwater beauty of the sea. All the locations feature calm waters and white sandy beaches.



    See another Gili island best for snorkeling in North side Lombok



    Gili Nanggu


    Gili Nanggu is the most beautiful island of these three Gili’s. There is almost nothing going on. its only one accommodation and one restaurant on the entire island. Snorkeling around Gili Nanggu, we will found aplenty of beautiful coral and colorful fish around. The beaches at Gili Nanggu are tropical white and you will find crystal clear blue water, where you can perfectly snorkel and swim. At Gili Nanggu we will found more fish and coral than on the other Gili islands.


    Gili Kedis



    Gili Kedis is the tiniest island! its is very small island and has the shape of a heart. You won’t find anything on the island, except from some trees in the middle.





    Gili Sudak



    After visiting Gili Nanggu and Gili Kedis, it is most likely that you will go for lunch at Gili Sudak. This island is a bit smaller than Gili Nanggu and known as the ‘lunch island’. You will find a couple of warungs (around four) where you can order all kind of local dishes like ‘Gado Gado’, ‘Nasi Goreng’, or ‘local fish’.
